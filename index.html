About Us

Your trusted partner in emergency medical services

Don Ambulance Services Limited

Based in Mbarara, Western Uganda, Don Ambulance Services Limited has been providing exceptional emergency medical transportation and critical care services since our establishment. We are committed to saving lives and ensuring the well-being of our community.

Our team of highly trained paramedics, emergency medical technicians, and support staff are dedicated to delivering prompt, professional, and compassionate care when every second counts.

We operate a modern fleet of fully equipped ambulances, each stocked with advanced medical equipment to handle various medical emergencies effectively.
